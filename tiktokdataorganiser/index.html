TikTok data export

Organize your export into a real folder structure

Drop in the JSON file from your TikTok data download (or the .zip it came in). Everything gets sorted into labeled category folders, and every direct message thread is pulled into its own separate folder — one file per person you've messaged, plus group chats kept apart from 1:1 chats. Nothing leaves your browser: the file is read and repackaged entirely on your machine.

What you'll get

organized_tiktok_data.zip ├── Direct Messages/ ← fully separate from everything else │ ├── Individual Chats/ │ │ ├── username1.txt │ │ ├── username2.txt │ │ └── ... │ └── Group Chats/ │ └── group_name.txt └── Categories/ ├── Comment/ ├── Likes and Favorites/ ├── Post/ ├── Profile And Settings/ ├── Your Activity/ ├── TikTok Live/ ├── TikTok Shop/ ├── Income+ Wallet/ └── Location Review/
Category folders mirror whatever sections your export actually contains — empty sections are skipped. Each chat is converted into a plain, readable transcript sorted oldest → newest.
Runs entirely client-side — your data is not sent anywhere.
